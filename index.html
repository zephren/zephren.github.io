Introduction

Will refer to JavaScript as JS.

[Talk about cognitive load and how I'm trying to keep that as minimal as possible, metaphors are meant to provide a familiar thing to attach concepts to, to help reduce cognitive load]

I'm writing this as a way to try and share my knowledge in my own way, where I have picked up some ideas that might help others learn JavaScript (and programming in general) more easily.

I will use terms that you may not be familiar with, but will link them to a glossary. It is not required that you know or understand these terms as they are introduced, but they will help you be able to communicate with others mor effectively. Other terms may be discussed more in later sections.
